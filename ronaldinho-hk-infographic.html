Navigating the FIFA World Cup 2026: Legally Astute Association

The strategy is not traditional "ambush marketing" but "ambassadorial storytelling." Ronaldinho, as an official FIFA Ambassador, shares his World Cup excitement from Hong Kong and China, creating a legitimate and powerful association while respecting FIFA's IP and local laws.

Core Principle: Ambassadorial Storytelling

Campaigns center on Ronaldinho's personal journey, his FIFA Ambassador role, and his experiences in HK/China. The World Cup is the exciting backdrop. This is "association by personality and official role," not by event IP infringement.
